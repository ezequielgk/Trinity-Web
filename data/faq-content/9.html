Minimum System Requirements:

Operating System:
• Linux (any distribution with Flatpak support)

Hardware:
• RAM: 2GB minimum (4GB recommended)
• Disk Space: 2GB free minimum
• Graphics: OpenGL 3.0 or Vulkan compatible
• Architecture: x86_64 (64-bit)

Software:
• Flatpak installed on the system
• Copy of Minecraft Bedrock x86-64

⚡ Performance: For an optimal experience, 8GB of RAM and a dedicated graphics card are recommended.
